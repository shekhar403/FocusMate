Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 5, 2025

FocusMate: Focus & Timer respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it.

1. No Data Collection

FocusMate does not collect, store, or share any personal or usage data. All information such as tasks, focus sessions, or preferences remains entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

2. Local Storage

Any information you enter (e.g., task names or timer settings) is saved locally on your device using Apple’s secure storage mechanisms. Deleting the app will remove this data.

3. No Third-Party Tracking

FocusMate does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party tracking tools.

4. Notifications

If you enable notifications, FocusMate only uses Apple’s local notification system to alert you when a focus session or break ends. No notification data is shared externally.
